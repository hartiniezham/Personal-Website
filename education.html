Tabika Kemas Batu 12 Gombak

My kindergarten

This is where I began my learning journey. My parents sent me here to study from 2005 to 2006. Because this kindergarten is close to my mother's workplace, she is usually the one who sends and picks me up from there. This place is a lot of fun; I learned a lot and played a lot of games. The teacher here is very lovely, and the food has always been delicious. I also have a large number of friends here. My memories of kindergarten are illustrated in the images below.

Sekolah Kebangsaan Sungai Pusu

My primary school

This is where I attended elementary school. Sekolah Kebangsaan is located at KM11, Jalan Gombak, Batu 7 Gombak, 53100 Kuala Lumpur, Selangor. The distance between my house and this school is quite far. My father would drop me off at school before he went to work. We rode for about 25 minutes on a motorcycle to get there. To go home, I will take the bus with other friends.

Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Gombak Setia

My secondary school

This is where I spent my high school years. This school is located at Jalan Seri Setia, Gombak Setia, 53100 Kuala Lumpur, Selangor. The school is a bit far from my house, so I'd take the bus as usual. Because of the distant location, I fell asleep on the bus several times and had to walk a long distance to school. All of those memories will stay with me forever.

Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Badang
My secondary school

My family relocated to Kota Bharu, Kelantan, in 2015 because my father desired to return to his hometown after decades of working in Kuala Lumpur. As a result, I had to transfer from SMK Gombak Setia to SMK Badang. It was initially awkward due to the different dialects. However, I was able to adapt to my surroundings over time.

Universiti Teknologi MARA Kampus Rembau, Negeri Sembilan
My University

In 2019, I began my studies at UiTM Rembau. In comparison to when I was in high school, the learning environment here is quite challenging. There are numerous assignments at UiTM that we must complete in order to receive a good pointer. Furthermore, the lecturer here is excellent because they always provides guidance if we are unsure about any aspect. Overall, I am grateful for the opportunity to study at university. Everything I learned while studying here will undoubtedly be useful in the future.
