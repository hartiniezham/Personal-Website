MY FAMILY

My Family

This is a photograph of my family. I am the youngest out of five brothers. I grew up in a loving family and lead a simple life. Our family had more than enough food, clothing, and a comfortable place to live. I am extremely grateful for this life.

My parents

Both of my parents are featured in the photo above. Che Mhd Ezham bin Amat is the name of my father. On February 20, 1960, he was born. He is 62 years old in 2022. My father grew up in the Kelantan town of Kota Bharu. He had moved to Kuala Lumpur at the age of 20 in search of a permanent job. He began working as a clerk in a business. Following that, he worked as a police officer in Malaysia's capital. He has received an offer to work at a Petronas company at KLCC after working for more than ten years. In his twenties, he met my mother in the neighbourhood where she worked. After many years of friendship, they decided to embark on the next stage of their lives by marrying. My father is a wonderful father. My father always concerned with the children's well-being and comfort. He carried out his responsibilities as a father very well.

Now I'd like to tell you about my mother. Jamnah Binti Sulaiman is the name of my mother. She was born in Pahang on November 6, 1959. My mother lived in Pahang with her family until she was 18 years old. Following that, my mother relocated to Kuala Lumpur in order to find work because the job opportunities there were greater than in her village. My mother works as a chef in a hospital in Kuala Lumpur. She has worked in the same place from the age of 18 to the age of 55. My mother is a strong woman who does an excellent job as a mother. She never failed to provide for his six children. Even if she is very busy at work, she will make sure that enough food is cooked at home.
